## Implementation

### Persona Detection
Personas are detected based on:
1. **Conversation signals** (questions asked, language used)
2. **Behavioral patterns** (hesitation, mentions of returns, etc.)
3. **Demographics** (age as proxy when signals unclear)

### Persona-Specific Messaging
Each conversation step uses persona-specific messaging:
- **Protector**: Direct, coverage-focused
- **Growth Seeker**: Educational, comparison-focused
- **Passive Aware**: Reassuring, guidance-focused

### Dynamic Education
Growth Seekers get additional education steps when they ask about returns/ULIPs, helping them understand why separation works better.
